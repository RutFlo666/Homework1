Fixed Gear Forever

News

Tenerife, March 27, 2025 — The sunny-coast-based company VeloxOne, known for its stylish and reliable fixed gear bikes, has unveiled a new collection called Neon Pulse, inspired by Tokyo street culture and a touch of cyberpunk aesthetics. The main feature of the new line is an innovative paint that changes color depending on air temperature and sunlight. “This is not just a bike, it’s a statement of identity,” says the brand’s creative director, Luis Jiménez. The company also announced a collaboration with local graffiti artists, whose artwork will decorate a limited edition of frames. Customers will even have the option to order custom airbrush designs with their own artwork.

VeloxOne stated that starting in May 2025, they plan to open pop-up showrooms in Berlin, Barcelona, and Amsterdam, where visitors can test ride the new bikes and enter to win a one-of-a-kind model as part of the #RideThePulse promo campaign.
